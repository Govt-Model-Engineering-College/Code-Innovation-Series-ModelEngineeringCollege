About HCP

Health Care Partner is an aadhar based national repository of medical history of the citizens giving the nation an effective healthcare management.Now all of your medical history in just one click.

HCP gives you:
     healthcare continuity
     healthcare information into public domain
     parity between all major branches of medicine
     a patient support system
     a decision support system for the health care fraternity

We, the Health Techies brings HCP for the nation this year 2020.
