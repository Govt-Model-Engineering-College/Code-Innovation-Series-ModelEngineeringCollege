RHEUMATOID ARTHRITIS

Pain or aching in more than one joint, Stiffness in more than one joint, Tenderness and swelling in more than one joint, The same symptoms on both sides of the body (such as in both hands or both knees), Weight loss, Fever, Fatigue or tiredness, Weakness are th symptoms of rheumatoid arthritis.

REMEDIES

1.Mix coriander powder with gingelly oil and rub it at the place of swelling
2.Heat castor leaves and tie it to the leg.
3.Grind leaves of drumstick and salt and rub it.

SPRAINS

Pain, Swelling, Bruising, Limited ability to move the affected joint, Hearing or feeling a "pop" in your joint at the time of injury are the symptoms of sprains.

REMEDIES

1.Grind boiled garlic and mix it in milk of half a coconut and rub it frequently.
2.Grind wild onion and rub it.
3.Grind root of hibiscus and rub it.
4.Grind kadaladi and turmeric and apply it in the position of sprain.
