DISEASES OF RESPIRATORY SYSTEM

1.COLD

A blocked or runny nose, a sore throat, headaches, muscle aches, coughs, sneezing, a raised temperature, pressure in your ears and face are the symptoms of cold.

REMEDIES

1.Cook crab with grinded cumin.
2.Squeeze two lemon in one glass of boiling water and drink it before going to sleep at night after adding sugar.
3.Drink gooseberry arishtam regularly.
4.Steam using Eucalyptus oil in hot water.
5.Crush gramboo and eat it along with honey.
6.Mix sugar candy in the juice of thulasi and eat it.
7.Drink kashayam made using ginger and pepper.
8.Drink hot milk with a pinch of turmeric powder and pepper powder.
9.Drink honey along with the juice of thulasi and shallot.

2.BRONCHITIS

Cough, Production of mucus (sputum), which can be clear, white, yellowish-gray or green in color - rarely, it may be streaked with blood, Fatigue, Shortness of breath, Slight fever and chills and Chest discomfort are the symptoms of bronchitis.

REMEDIES

1.Eat two dhanwantharam pills in the morning in empty stomach and at 6'o clock in the evening along with hot cumin water.
2.Eat gorochanadi pills along with ginger juice three times a day after meal.
3.Powder pepper and sugar candy and eat it regularly.

3.ASTHAMA

Cough, Production of mucus (sputum), which can be clear, white, yellowish-gray or green in color - rarely, it may be streaked with blood, Fatigue, Shortness of breath, Slight fever and chills, Chest discomfort are the symptoms of asthama.

REMEDIES

1.Drink ginger juice along with honey.
2.Mix jaggery with mustard oil and eat it regularly.
3.Powder pepper and sesame and eat it along with jaggery and honey.
4.Drink kayyonni juice along with powdered pepper.
5.Mix the juice of ginger along with thippili and himalyan salt and eat it.
6.Eat the juice of ash gourd along with crushed leaves of drumsticks.
7.Mix a spoon of honey and a hen egg along with milk and drink it.
8.Mix two teaspoon of thulasi juice along with honey and eat it regularly in empty stomach.
9.Grind touch-me-not and drink it regularly for 15 days along with coconut milk.

4.PNEUMONIA

Cough, Production of mucus (sputum), which can be clear, white, yellowish-gray or green in color - rarely, it may be streaked with blood, Fatigue, Shortness of breath, Slight fever and chills, Chest discomfort are the symptoms of pneumonia.

REMEDIES

1.Mix garlic juice in mustard oil and rub it on the back and chest. Also drink milk mixed with 2 ml of garlic juice.
2.Steam using camphor.
3.Rub turpentine on the chest.
4.Drink lot of lukewarm drinks.
